ruby-hyper-v

A Ruby helper for administering Hyper-V virtual machines.

Right now, its best trick is listing the virtual machines on a host: 

require 'hyper-v'
server = HyperV::Server.new('MyServer')
server.virtual_machines.each do |vm|
  puts vm.name
end
